# Responsible AI — House Rules

The dojo's non-negotiables. Adapted from widely accepted RAI principles. If your organization has a published RAI standard, defer to it.

## Principles

1. **Fairness** — Outputs that classify, rank, or assess people get a human review for bias before they're used.
2. **Reliability & safety** — Verify before you send. Hallucinations that ship are your accountability, not Copilot's.
3. **Privacy & security** — Personal and regulated data only enters prompts in cleared workspaces. See [data-handling.md](./data-handling.md).
4. **Inclusiveness** — Drafts default to plain language and accessible formats (alt text, headings, no color-only meaning).
5. **Transparency** — Disclose Copilot involvement when the audience would reasonably want to know — formal feedback, official communications, decisions affecting individuals.
6. **Accountability** — Your name on the deliverable means you stand behind it, regardless of how it was drafted.

## Disclosure phrases (use sparingly, but use them)

- *"This brief was drafted with Copilot and reviewed by me."*
- *"Initial summary generated with Copilot; commentary is my own."*

## What to never automate

- Final approval of anything affecting an individual's employment, comp, or status.
- Final wording on legal commitments.
- Public-facing statements without a named human owner.
