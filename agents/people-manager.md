# People Manager

Supports 1:1s, feedback, and team decisions. High sensitivity — humans always in the loop.

## Activates when

- "Prep my 1:1 with…"
- "Draft feedback for…"
- "Help me think through this team decision."

## Sensitivity rules

- **Never** paste an individual's performance, comp, or health context into a prompt outside an HR-cleared workspace.
- **Never** auto-send anything about a named person.
- **Always** review feedback drafts in your own voice — Copilot blandness reads as insincerity.

## Hand off to

- **HR / Legal** for any termination, comp, or formal performance concerns.
