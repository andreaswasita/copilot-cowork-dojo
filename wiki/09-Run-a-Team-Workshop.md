# 09 — Run a Team Workshop

> ⏱ **30 minutes to plan · 4 hours to run** · 🎯 **Get a team to "we use this every week"**

The dojo travels well in a workshop. One half-day with 6–24 people produces shippable deliverables and a habit-forming routine.

## Outcomes you're aiming for

By the end of the workshop, every participant should:

1. Have **shipped one real deliverable** through the full pipeline.
2. Recognize and apply at least 4 core kata.
3. Have logged at least one lesson in `tasks/lessons.md`.
4. Know where the **sensitivity line** is in their context.
5. Commit to one habit for next week.

## The agenda

The dojo ships a ready-to-run agenda. Don't reinvent it.

→ [facilitator/agendas/half-day.md](../facilitator/agendas/half-day.md)

## The facilitator's responsibilities

→ [facilitator/workshop-guide.md](../facilitator/workshop-guide.md)

Highlights:

- **Real work only.** No toy examples. Ask each participant to bring **one real deliverable** they owe in the next 7 days.
- **Pair people up.** Solo work in workshops becomes lurking. Pairs force engagement.
- **Don't lecture the framework — run it.** Use *your own* deliverable as the demo in Block 1.
- **Defend the SHIP gate.** Under deadline pressure, people skip. Don't let them.
- **Make LEARN visible.** Have every participant write a lesson live in Block 5 — out loud, on a shared screen.

## Pre-work to send 3 days before

```
Subject: Cowork Dojo workshop — quick prep (~15 min)

Hi team —

Three things to bring on Thursday:

1) One real deliverable you owe in the next 7 days
   (recap, brief, status, memo, email — not a slide deck full of charts)

2) Skim these two pages (~10 min total):
   - <link to README.md>
   - <link to wiki/04-The-Pipeline-Explained.md>

3) Make sure Microsoft 365 Copilot is working in your account today
   (a 2-minute test will save us time in the room)

See you Thursday.
```

## What to prepare on the day

- [ ] [`.github/copilot-instructions.md`](../.github/copilot-instructions.md) pinned in the demo workspace.
- [ ] [Prompt library](../prompts/library.md) printed or on a side screen.
- [ ] Empty `tasks/lessons.md` open on a shared screen for Block 5.
- [ ] HR-cleared workspace ready in case someone's deliverable touches personal data.
- [ ] Belt printouts (informal — fun award at the end).

## Common workshop pitfalls

| Pitfall | Fix |
|---|---|
| Someone brings a fake / toy deliverable | Send them back to their inbox during the break |
| Pairs skip CLARIFY because "we already know what we want" | Make them write the frame *out loud* before drafting |
| One participant dominates Q&A | Use "ask in pairs first, share one question" |
| Group skips REVIEW because the draft "reads fine" | Run a 5-lens demo on a *known-bad* draft you prepared |
| LEARN gets cut for time | Cut a discussion segment, never cut LEARN |

## After the workshop

Schedule three things in the calendar **before everyone leaves the room**:

1. **30-min office hours, weekly, for 4 weeks** — drop-in for stuck moments.
2. **30-min lesson review, end of week 4** — promote first patterns.
3. **Belt-up retro, end of month 1** — what's stuck, what hasn't, what to add.

Without these, workshop energy decays in 7 days.
